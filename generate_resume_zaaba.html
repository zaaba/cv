About Profile & Focus Areas

Ts. Dr. Zaaba Ahmad is a lecturer and AI researcher specialising in natural language processing, image processing and computer vision, with a strong emphasis on mental health support systems and applied AI for public-sector and smart city contexts.

His work spans AI-powered counselling systems (AIMI, MAYA), AI reception and service automation, and AI-driven digital twin approaches for infrastructure monitoring and city planning. He is actively involved as consultant and think-tank member for national and municipal initiatives, including Putrajaya 2050.
